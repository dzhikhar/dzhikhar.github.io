Dmitrii M. Zhikharevich

Postdoctoral Research Fellow, VALUE-VACC · Department of Political Science, University of Vienna

ORCID · Email · University profile · LinkedIn · PDF CV

I’m an economic sociologist and social theorist researching the interplay of economic valuation and expertise across finance, public policy, and public health.

Research interests: social theory; economic sociology; historical sociology; valuation studies; science and technology studies (STS); sociology of expertise.

  • Since 02/2023 — University of Vienna, Department of Political Science (Austria): Postdoctoral Research Fellow, VALUE-VACC.
  • 05/2022–09/2022 — Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, Department of Sociology & Anthropology (Israel): Visiting Fellow.
  • 09/2020–05/2022 — European University at St Petersburg, STS Centre & Department of Sociology (Russia): Research Fellow.
  • 10/2019–07/2020 — London School of Economics and Political Science, Department of Accounting (United Kingdom): LSE Fellow.

Education

  • 2014–2019 — PhD in Accounting, London School of Economics and Political Science. Dissertation: Heuristics of Capital: A Historical Sociology of U.S. Venture Capitalism, 1946–1968.
